The Story

waterproof ✦ non-tarnishing ✦ durable

Lasting Quality

I aim to create jewelry that can be effortlessly lived in. I make most of my pieces using stainless steel because it is a corrosion-resistant metal that is waterproof, hypoallergenic, and will not tarnish under normal conditions. It requires less maintenance, resulting in less stress. You can shower or sweat with it on without fear of it changing color. Stainless steel is significantly harder than gold or silver and more scratch-resistant, making it perfect for everyday wear.

one-of-a-kind ✦ natural ✦ genuine

No Synthetics

I use natural, genuine grade A-AAA gemstones in my designs because I believe nothing can compete with the radiance and authenticity of nature. Each gemstone is one-of-a-kind with its own distinct characteristics and energy, which are qualities that man-made synthetic gemstones simply cannot replicate. The subtle variations of natural gemstones add a personal touch, making each piece uniquely yours.

unique ✦ versatile ✦ illuminating

Design Philosophy

My goal is to create high-quality jewelry that is captivating and versatile. I draw inspiration from minimalist, mystical, and soft grunge styles, blending timeless elegance with industrial edge. This fusion of contrasting elements creates depth and character that reflects the complexity of human nature while illuminating individual expression. Each piece is thoughtfully designed to embody a deeper meaning and is aimed to uplift the wearer, both inside and out.
